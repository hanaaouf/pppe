About Me

I am a first-year Control and Automation Engineering student at the National Polytechnic School of Algiers. I completed two years of preparatory classes in Science and Technology at ENPA, ranking among the top 10% of my cohort.

Alongside robotics, I have a strong passion for mathematics, computer science, and analytical thinking. I enjoy modeling systems, analyzing problems, and applying theoretical concepts to real engineering challenges.

Curious, ambitious, and dynamic, I am motivated to continuously deepen my knowledge in automation, embedded systems, and intelligent control by combining solid theory with hands-on engineering practice.
